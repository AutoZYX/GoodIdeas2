Rosen 的四个关键词 Rosen's Four Keywords

探究 (Investigation)、解读 (Interpretation)、发明 (Invention)、合作 (Co-operation)。来自英国桂冠儿童诗人 Michael Rosen 的经典育儿理念。 Investigation, Interpretation, Invention, Co-operation. Classic parenting philosophy from UK Children's Laureate Michael Rosen.

🚗

场景驱动的育儿 Scenario-Driven Parenting

不是用一套"正确理论"套用到所有时刻，而是在每个具体场景中观察、判断、回应——就像自动驾驶的场景测试方法一样。 Instead of applying one "correct theory" to every moment, observe, assess, and respond in each specific scenario — just like scenario-based testing in autonomous driving.

🤖

AI：第三个学习伙伴 AI: The Third Learning Partner

不是"AI取代父母"，而是"父母 + 孩子 + AI"的三角关系。每章附带可直接使用的 AI Prompt。 Not "AI replacing parents," but a triangle of parent + child + AI. Each chapter includes a ready-to-use AI prompt.

自动化等级 × 育儿 Automation Levels × Parenting

每个父母每天都在做"场景判断"：这个场景下，我该介入到什么程度？ Every parent makes "scenario judgments" daily: how much should I intervene in this situation?

L0
人类全程驾驶Full human driving
你替孩子做一切决定You make every decision for the child
L1
辅助驾驶Driver assistance
你扶着他骑自行车You hold the bike while they ride
L2
部分自动Partial automation
他自己做作业，你在旁边看着They do homework; you watch nearby
L3
有条件自动Conditional automation
他自己去便利店买酸奶They go to the corner shop alone
L4
高度自动High automation
他自己安排周末怎么过They plan their own weekend
L5
完全自主Full autonomy
他成年了，完全独立决策They're grown up, fully independent

张玉新 Yuxin Zhang

吉林大学副教授 | 自动驾驶安全研究者 | Lucky的爸爸 Associate Professor, Jilin University | AD Safety Researcher | Lucky's Dad

研究自动驾驶安全的人，在英国独自带娃一年后，发现育儿和自动驾驶是同一件事。白天在实验室研究"车在什么场景下该由人接管"，晚上在家琢磨"孩子在什么场景下该由我介入"。这本书是他和5岁儿子Lucky在纽卡斯尔这一年的探究日志。 A researcher in autonomous driving safety who, after a year of solo parenting in the UK, realized that parenting and self-driving are the same problem. By day he studies when a car should hand control to the human; by night he figures out when to step in with his kid. This book is the field journal of his year in Newcastle with his 5-year-old son Lucky.
